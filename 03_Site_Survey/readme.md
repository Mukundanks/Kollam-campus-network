# Site Survey

## Overview
 A site survey was conducted with the help of aerial imaging and physical site knowledge (alumini), to understand the project in a bigger picture.

 The core agenda of the survey was to identify:
   * Geographical location
   * Boundaries
   * Existing network infrastructure
   * Road network
   * Possible obstacles
   * Distance from core
   * Future expansion possibilities
 The preliminary inspection showed that many institutions have shared boundaries, and 4 institutions are located close to the railway property.

## Survey Methodology
  Due to the highly conceptual nature of this project, a physical site visit is not possible. Instead, it was conducted based on data from Maps, aerial imaging, and most importantly my knowldege of the locatoin when I was a student of one of the institutions. 

## Geographical Overview
  There are many reasons why I have chosen Kollam as my project location. This area is well-konwn to me, and being an alumni of one of the institutions, I have a good knowledge of the location. This location has around 9 institutions within the radius of less than 1KM, which makes an interconnect highly feasible.
